Biography

Kureen Paige

I am a freelance photographer specializing in capturing special events, senior pictures, portraits, and sporting events. My journey in photography began as a hobby while capturing moments at my daughter's cheerleading competitions. The more I immersed myself in this passion, the more diverse events I covered. From weddings and family reunions to office events and birthday parties, I have experience in a wide range of photography projects. If it's interesting and challenging, I am eager to take on the task. Additionally, I have ventured into video production and recently started recording drone videos. Thank you for visiting my site, and please consider recommending my services to a friend.
